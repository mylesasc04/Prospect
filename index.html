About Us

We're all about providing an opportunity to the unfortunate. Propsect NYC seeks to find accessible scholarships towards minorities. We also use a rating system to determine the best scholarship for all students. Scholarships with a high success rate and positive comments equates to a better ranking and visa versa. Through All Star Code, we are able to create a website that helps the community save money and make higher education accessible. Through this website, we want everyone to get a scholarship.
